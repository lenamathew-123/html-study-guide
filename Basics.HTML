Some tags in HTML

  1. Heading tag
    2.
    Used to display headings in HTML
    h1 (most important)
    h3
    h2
    h4
    h5
    h6 (least important)

  2. Paragraph tag

    3. <p>This is a sample paragraph </p>

  3. Anchor Tag
    4.

    Used to add links to your page

    <a href="https://google.com"> Google </a>

  4. Image tag
    5.
    <img src="/image.png" alt="Logo" width="100" height="50">

  5. Bold, Italic & Underline Tags
    6.

    Used to highlight text in your page

    • <b> Bold </b>
    • <i> Italic </i>
    • <u> Underline </u>


  6. Big & Small Tags
    7.

    Used to display big & small text on your page

    • <big> Big word </big>
    • <small> small word </small>

  7. Hr Tag

    8. Used to display a horizontal ruler, used to separate content

    <hr>
  8. Subscript & Superscript Tag

    9. Used to display a horizontal ruler, used to separate content

    • <sub> subscript </sub>
    • <sup> Superscript </sup>

  9. Pre Tag

    10. Used to display text as it is (without ignoring spaces & next line)

    <pre> This is a sample text.</pre>
